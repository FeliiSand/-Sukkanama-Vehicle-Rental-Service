Who We Are?

The idea of starting sukkanama Vehicles arises with a vision of providing travellers with their desired rental vehicles. For many traditional car rentals, vehicles offered are usually overpriced or, in some cases, unregulated. sukkanama aims to provide a platform where its members can easily access better, and unique vehicles at more affordable prices. No more guessing whether your next vehicle is what you saw online!.

sukkanama is also where you can rent out your vehicles when not used. A simple streamlined listing process makes earning passively from your vehicles easier than you could imagine! List your vehicles with us and let your vehicles work for you!.

At Sukkanama, we take immense pride in introducing our innovative vehicle renting system, built on a foundation of excellence. Our unwavering commitment to providing exceptional service sets us apart in the industry. We extend a warm invitation to you to join us and discover the remarkable difference our new system makes in your travel journey.
